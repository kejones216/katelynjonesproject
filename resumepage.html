EXPERIENCE

Katelyn has been performing with The Madison Theater at Molloy College for four years, featuring in various cabaret stage productions during the theatre's performance season.

Professional credits include The White Rabbit in a kid-friendly stage production of Alice in Wonderland directed by Broadway legend Baayork Lee, performed at The Madison Theater at Molloy College. A featured ensemble member of a production of Les Miserables directed by Katherine Carter, produced by Spiro Productions, performed at professional theater The Meadowbrook Theater in Detroit, Michigan.

Katelyn has also had the opportunity to perform as a circus performer in two music festivals produced by Bang On! NYC music production company. She was able to use various circus skills to be an interactive performer for two festivals.

Katelyn has also had the opportunity to work as an intern at two different companies. The Summer of 2015, she had the opportunity to work with her mentors at Kaleidoscope Studio for the Performing Arts, where she worked as a Production Assistant for a small musical for a children's summer theater day camp. The summer of 2016, she worked as a General Management Assistant for Aruba Production Company in NYC. She was right hand assistant to managers of the production company, which was responsible for the management of a touring C.S. Lewis play.
