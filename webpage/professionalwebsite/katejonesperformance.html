A little about Katelyn...

Katelyn Jones was born and raised just outside Detroit, Michigan. As a creative young girl, she leared how to play the piano. She loved to sing in her basement to her favorite pop tunes. Formally a gymnast, this little girl needed a new creative outlet. She starting to sing a dance around the age of eleven, and has since never stopped!

A few of katelyns hobbies includes various types of performance art. She's a skilled performance hula hooper, able to do advance tricks and combos with various dance props like a hula hoop, silk streamers, and poi. In her spare time, you can catch her painting, and dancing with her hula hoop outside!

Katelyn chose to attend CAP21 Musical Theater Conservatory, and will graduate in the spring of 2018 with a Bachelors of Fine Arts in Theater Arts. She got to study under the direction of working broadway actors and composers, like Larry Arancio, Amy Francis, Randy Redd, David Hibbard, Amanda Flynn, and many more
